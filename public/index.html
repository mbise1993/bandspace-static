What is Bandspace?

Messaging

The only messaging app your band needs. Use channels to organize your discussions so that important announcements don't get lost amongst the memes.

Files and Storage

Tired of using various cloud storage providers to keep up with your demos, mix revisions, promo pics, designs, etc.? We are too. That's why Bandspace comes with a built-in file manager to keep track of all your band's things.

Calendar

Bandspace gives you a shared calendar so that everyone stays on the same page. No more asking "When are we practicing again?" or "What day is the next show?".

Social Media

From scheduling posts to coming up with new and creative ways to connect with your fans, Bandspace has you covered. Link all your band's social media accounts to collaboratively create and manage your ideas.
